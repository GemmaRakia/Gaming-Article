Why is it played?

Street fighter has accuired a large player base over it's inception other three decades ago. But what makes SFV different from the rest.

SFV is the first one to use unreal engine which allows them to make graphical improvements to the game such as adding physics to clothing and hair. This is why almost all the characters something in their design that would be impacted by the characters movemnts(to show of the new physics).

They also added "Role Back Netcode" which has quickly become a must in modern fighting games. This is due to the fact that more tournaments are being held online as well as the that is also how the general playerbase chose to play the game. Role Back Nectcode works by recording the events of a match whilst its happening; this is done in casse of the event of packet loss, the game would simply go back to a previouse state in which there was no interference (hence the name role back).
