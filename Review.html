Reveiw

I believe that Street Fighter 5 is a good game even if you have never played a fighting game before as there are plent of characters to enjoy and you don't need to fully understand them to find enjoyment in them. However, if you do learn how the game works you will be treated with a deep and engaging game of attrition as you subvert your opponents expectations and assert your dominance. Please Note that they just realesed their final update as they prepare for street fighter 6.
