What's needed?

Street Fighter 5 can be played on PC(via steam), PS4 and PS5. If your chosen platform is PC it is recomended that you have the follow: Intel Core i5-4690K @3.50GHz or AMD FX-9370; 8 GB RAM; NVIDIA® GeForce® GTX 960 or AMD Radeon R7 370; DirectX Version 11; and a stable internet connection. It is also advised to play with a controller over a keyboard as it is generally much easier that way. You can also play on a fight stick but they are more expensive and can only be used in fighting games.
